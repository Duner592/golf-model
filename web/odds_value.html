How this page works

Beta status means this view is new for 2026 and should be reviewed alongside prediction accuracy, calibration, and betting history before drawing conclusions.

Implied win is the bookmaker price turned into a probability. A 40-1 price implies about 2.4% before margin.

Win edge is model win % minus implied win %. Positive means the model is higher than the price implies.

E/W value combines the win and place legs. Top 10 places use the model Top 10 directly; Top 7/8 places are estimated from Top 10 and marked as estimates.

EV is the long-run expected return if the model probability is right.
EV = decimal odds x model probability - 1
E/W EV = (win EV + place EV) / 2

Included bets are pre-tournament `E/W` and `Outright` only, so the board stays aligned to the tournament outright model.

Archive view uses saved 2026 archive leaderboards, so previous weeks are scored against the prediction snapshot stored for that event.
